Sophi Knight - Age, Family, Bio

Child actress turned model working with the clothing line Youth Apparel who continued her acting career. She'd later join the cast of the show 2nd Generation.

Before Fame

Some of her earliest acting credits came in 2006 when she was 7 or 8 years old.

Trivia

Her feature film credits include The Haunting in Connecticut and Walled In.

Family Life

Her father owned an animation studio called Red Rover.

Associated With

She and David Knoll were both cast in 2nd Generation.
