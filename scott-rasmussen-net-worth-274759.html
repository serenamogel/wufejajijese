Scott Rasmussen net worth: Scott Rasmussen is an American political analyst and digital media entrepreneur who has a net worth of $100 million. Scott Rasmussen was born in Eglin Air Force Base, Florida in March 1956. He is the publisher of the public opinion website ScottRasmussen.com which debuted in 2018. Rasmussen is also the Editor-at-Large for Ballotpedia and writers their Number of the Day feature. He founded Rasmussen Reports and was president and a pollster for 10 years until he left in 2013. He previously co-founded the ESPN sports network with his father Bill Rasmussen. He also founded the Rasmussen Media Group where he was an author, political analyst, columnist, and public speaker. Scott Rasmussen graduated from DePauw University and Wake Forest University and is a Senior Fellow at The King's College. He is a New York Times bestselling author and has released the books Solving the Budget Crisis: Hope for America's Future, A Better Deal: Social Security Choice, Mad As Hell: How the Tea Party Movement Is Fundamentally Remarking Our Two-Party System, In Search of Self-Governance, The People's Money: How Voters Will Balance the Budget and Eliminate the Federal Debt, and Politics Has Failed: America Will Not.
