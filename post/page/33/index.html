Is diagonals of rectangle are equal?

The diagonals of a rectangle are of equal length. The two diagonals of a rectangle are the hypotenuse of the two right angled triangles. Since, the height and base of the two triangles are equal, by Pythagoras Theorem, the hypotenuse of the triangles are also equal.
