Mick Foley Puts Eric Bischoff’s Butt In A Seat

WWE Hall of Famer Mick Foley has put up a post on his Facebook page commenting on how he’s buried the hatchet with his former WCW boss Eric Bischoff. Eric infamously spoiled Mick Foley’s big WWF World Title win from RAW on January 4, 1999 by instructing WCW commentator Tony Schivone to announce it on Nitro and say, “That’ll put butts in the seats.”


Eric and Mick were both at the Hamilton Comic-Con in Canada over the weekend. While they were there Eric made an exclusive video with Mick where they discussed Frank The Clown’s controversial promo from Freelance Wrestling this weekend. You can view it in the embedded video player below.
