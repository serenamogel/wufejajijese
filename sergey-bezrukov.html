A glance at Sergey Bezrukov

Sergey Bezrukov is famous among the public as a Russian actor. He is a handsome man who is 5feet 8inches (173cm) tall who has brown hair color and weighs around 75Kg.

He was born on October 18, 1973, in Moscow, Russia. His nationality is Russian and holds Libra as his birth sign.

Family, and Early life

Sergey was born and raised in Moscow by his parents. He has got a lovely and supportive family. He spent his childhood in Moscow. Vitali Bezrukov is his father who is an actor by profession and Natalya Mikhailovna Bezrukova is his mother who is a housewife.

He did not face any difficulties in his childhood days for good education and for a quality of life because he belongs to well-sophisticated family background. 

Education

He is well educated and finished his early education at a private school in Moscow. Later he joined the Moscow Art Theater School of the studio and graduated there in 1994. 

However, he has not disclosed any sort of information about his majors.

Who is the wife of Sergey Bezrukov now?

He is currently in a married relationship with Anna Matison. They married each other after dating for several months. The romantic couple tied their knot in the year 2016 in a private ceremony by gathering their family members and close friends. 

And from that moment they are happy with their relationship and are still together at the moment.

Father of three children

Sergey is the father of three lovely kids Maria Bezrukova, Ivan Bezrukov, and Alexandra Bezrukova. These are the kids from his both wives.

Past relationship 

Before marrying Anna Matison he was married to Irina Bezrukova who is an actress by profession. They married in the year 2000 but their relationship could not last long and in 2015 they got separated from each other. They are now living their personal life.

Professional life and Career

He is an outstanding actor who started his acting career by portraying his role as Chibis in Nocturne for Drum and motorcycle. He has appeared in many films and TV series with different roles as  Irakliy in The Irony of Fate 2, Kappel in Admiral, Sumarokov in High-Security Vacation, Sasha Belov in Law of Lawless, Sergei Esenin in Esenin, Vladimir Vysotsky in Vysotsky and Thank you for being alive.

He has also done a biopic movie in his career. He is still working in the Russian film industry.

Achievements and Awards of Sergey Bezrukov?

He has won many titles in his career for his remarkable performances in his acting career.

  • He won the title of Golden Eagle Awards as Best Television Actor of Russia in 2005.
  • He also has the title of MTV Movie Awards as Best Actor of Russia 2008 and won the People's Artist of Russian Federation in the same year.
