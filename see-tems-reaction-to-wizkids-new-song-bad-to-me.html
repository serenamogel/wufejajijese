See Tems Reaction To Wizkids New Song, Bad To Me

2022 BET Award Best International Act winner, Temilade Openiyi, better known by her stage name, Tems has reacted to Wizkid’s latest output, ‘Bad To Me‘.

The multiple award winning Nigerian singer, songwriter and performing artiste has taken to social media to react to Starboy Entertainment head, Wizkid’s song, ‘Bad To Me’ which was released on Wednesday, September 14, 2022.

The young beautiful songstress and ‘Crazy Tings’ crooner whose career elevated after her contribution to Wizkid’s ‘Essence’ and the connection between the two has remains healthy has joined the tall list of celebrities to laud Wizkid’s ‘Bad To Me’.
