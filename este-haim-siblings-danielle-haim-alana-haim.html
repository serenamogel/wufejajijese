Este Haim siblings: Danielle Haim, Alana Haim

Este Haim is an American musician who is best known as a member of the American pop-rock band Haim, along with her two sisters, Alana and Danielle, who, in 2020, received a Grammy Award for Album of the Year nomination for their third album, Women in Music Pt. III. She was born in Los Angeles, California, the United States on March 14, 1986, to her parents Mordechai Haim and Donna Haim. She is 36 years old.

Este Haim siblings: Danielle Haim, Alana Haim

Este Haim is from a family of three siblings – all girls and happens to be the first child and daughter of her parents, hence, she has two younger sisters who have been identified as Alana and Danielle Haim.

The siblings founded the American pop-rock band Haim, and they have been active since 2007.

Danielle Haim is the second daughter of her parents. She was born on February 16, 1989 (age 33 years), in Los Angeles, California, in the United States. She doubles as an American actress known for Licorice Pizza, Haim: The Steps, and Haim: Summer Girl, just to mention a few. 

Alana Mychal Haim on the other hand is the youngest of the three siblings. She was born December 15, 1991 (age 30 years), in Los Angeles, California, United States. She is an American musician and actress.
