Shop Thomas Rhett's Favorite Hats 2024

For Thomas Rhett Akins, every day is a hat day. The country star has made a habit of wearing hats, so much so that he posted an Instagram picture of his evergrowing cap collection that he brought on tour last summer. “Left for 2 hours,” the caption wrote. “Lauren Akins [his wife] went to town sorting my hats on the bus. Anyone else do this??” No, Thomas, not everyone has dozens of hats for any and every occasion. But we wish we did!

From on stage to a chill day at home with the kids, we can safely confirm that the singer's hats are a part of his capsule wardrobe. So, if you’re jealous of this revelation like we are, how can you start stacking up an epic hat reserve similar to Rhett’s? We put together a list of some of the top caps he wears so you could die a happy man (sorry folks—we couldn’t continue without at least one song mention).

YETI Mid Pro Trucker Hat

Yeti Mid Pro Trucker Hat

Yeti Mid Pro Trucker Hat

Camo is a tried and true pattern for any country singer out there — and it’s on sale for 25% off! This YETI hat comes in a brown camo mix (as Rhett frequently sports on stage) or the classic green camo. Plus, the material is advertised to be made from 100% recycled fishing nets.

Love One 3D Embroidered Trucker Hat

Love One 3D Embroidered Trucker Hat

If you don’t act fast enough — there are less than fifty left in stock! — this embroidered foam trucker hat is just as easy to pair with your favorite outfit.

Ford Motor Co. Baseball Cap

American Needle Ford Motors Co. Baseball Cap

American Needle Ford Motors Co. Baseball Cap

There’s something about a solid blue baseball cap that screams nostalgia. Ford has a slew of hats available on Amazon with prices varying — so we’ll let you check out Rhett’s pick and make a determination of your favs from there (we like the bay blue one!).

Titeleist Tour Stretch Golf Hat

Titleist Tour Stretch Golf Hat

Titleist Tour Stretch Golf Hat

You didn’t think we could round up a list of hats and not mention golf, did you? The country singer often sports a white and black Titleist cap that is available in sizes small to extra large. Perfect for a day on the range or thousands of screaming fans — whichever matches your lifestyle!
