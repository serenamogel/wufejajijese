Pharrell Williams net worth: How much is he worth

Pharrell Williams was born on April 5, 1973 in Virginia Beach, Virginia, United States by Carolyn Williams, and Pharaoh Williams. Pharrell Williams has six siblings namely; Pharoah Williams III, Cato Williams, Hope Williams, Psolomon Williams, Carrie Parks, Unitra Laville, and David Williams.

Pharrell Lanscilo Williams is an American record producer, rapper, singer songwriter, and fashion designer. He is also a close colleague of Chad Hugo, he formed the hip hop and R&B production duo the Neptunes in the early 1990s, with whom he has produced songs for various recording artists.
