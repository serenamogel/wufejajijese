Get to Know Hallie Gnatovich, Josh Gates's Wife - Biography, Age, Wiki, Net Worth, and More


Discover more about Hallie Gnatovich, also recognized as Josh Gates’s spouse, including her bio, age, Wiki, net worth, and other updates.

Hallie Gnatovich Biography & Profile Summary

Hallie Gnatovich Height & Weight

Curious about Hallie Gnatovich’s height and weight? Her height reaches approximately 5 feet 5 inches, with a weight of about 51 kgs. Additionally, her chest-waist-hip measurements are 32-25-34 inches, and she has brown eyes and hair.

Hallie Gnatovich Education

Hallie Gnatovich earned a BA in Theatre from Oberlin College in 2004, followed by a Master’s degree in marriage and family counseling therapy from Alliant International University in 2010. Between 2009 and 2012, she embarked on her transition to a career as a therapist, holding positions at the Japanese center and the Jewish Family Center.

She is a Lady of Ambition and established her counseling firm in 2009 in West Hollywood, California. Apart from her firm, she also worked as a consultant for MedAvante-ProPhase since 2008, aiding in the training of pharmaceutical researchers in DSM symptomatology.

Hallie Gnatovich Career

  • Hallie Gnatovich is active as both a therapist and actress.
  • She is widely recognized as the wife of TV host and explorer Josh Gates.
  • Hallie currently runs a website called www.halliegtherapy.com and practices kundalini yoga.
  • Her therapy focus includes aiding individuals with mental health challenges, prioritizing assistance to young people, mothers dealing with childcare issues, actors, writers, and struggling LGBT couples.
  • She also dabbled in acting, with a minor role on Syfy’s Destination Truth, where she played a researcher.
  • After graduating from Oberlin College, she worked in various production crews before her breakthrough role in the short film Archer House in 2007. She secured a recurring character role as a researcher in Destination Truth.
  • Despite her success in acting, Hallie shifted her focus to psychology and therapy during her time on Destination Truth, ultimately pursuing counseling and therapy.
  • From 2009 to 2012, she worked with Jenesse Center and obtained a certification in Mindfulness before earning her master’s degree in Marriage and Family Therapy and starting her own company, Hallie G. Therapy.

Hallie Gnatovich Net Worth 2023

What is Hallie Gnatovich’s net worth? She is estimated to have a net worth of around $1.3 million as of 2023, amassed from her acting career and therapy business.

Hallie Gnatovich Husband & Marriage

Who is Hallie Gnatovich’s Husband? She is married to Josh Gates, an American TV presenter and actor. They met during the production of Destination Truth and tied the knot on September 13, 2014. The couple shares two children, Owen Gates and their daughter.

Their relationship was initially kept private, and their wedding was an intimate affair. Hallie introduced their son to the world via a tweet, and she surprised Josh with the news of her second pregnancy while he was filming Secrets of Christopher Columbus.
