Matt Hughes Death: Tributes For The Brave Storm Chaser

Matt received numerous heartfelt condolences from Discovery producers and friends, and family.

His family released a statement a few days after the news of his death was made public.

The statement stated that “Matt Hughes was genuinely a wonderful and caring person with a warm and loving soul, in the perception of his various relatives, friends, and coworkers.

The Hughes family went on to state that their intention was never to hide the truth about Matt’s passing to mislead the public.

But instead, to give family space to grieve privately and safeguard the several young children this tragedy has impacted.

They also highlighted the devastating effects of depression and noted that it is a treatable condition despite its huge incidence.
