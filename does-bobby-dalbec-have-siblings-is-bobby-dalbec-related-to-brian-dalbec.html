Bobby Dalbec is an American professional baseball corner infielder for the Boston Red Sox of Major League Baseball.

Robert Vernon Dalbec was born on June 29, 1995, in Seattle, Washington, United States. He attended Legend High School in Parker, Colorado, where he played for the school’s baseball team as a shortstop and a pitcher.

Bobby Dalbec was not selected in the Major League Baseball (MLB) draft because of his strong commitment to attend the University of Arizona where he went on to enrol as a general studies major.

Bobby Dalbec enrolled at Arizona to play college baseball for the Arizona Wildcats. He played collegiate summer baseball for the Orleans Firebirds of the Cape Cod Baseball League (CCBL) following his freshman season in 2014.

Bobby Dalbec was selected by the Boston Red Sox in the fourth round of the 2016 Major League Baseball (MLB) draft. He signed with the Red Sox and received a $650,000 signing bonus. Bobby Dalbec made his professional debut with the Class A Short Season Lowell Spinners and also made his MLB debut that day, against the Washington Nationals on August 30, 2020.
