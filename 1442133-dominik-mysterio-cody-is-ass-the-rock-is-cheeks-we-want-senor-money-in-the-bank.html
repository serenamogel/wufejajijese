Dominik Mysterio: Cody Is Ass, The Rock Is Cheeks; We Want Seor Money In The Bank

The entire landscape of WrestleMania 40 changed this past Friday night on WWE SmackDown. The winner of the 2024 Men’s Royal Rumble Cody Rhodes showed up on the Blue Brand to confront Roman Reigns. But things took an unexpected turn as The American Nightmare chose to finish his story a little later and let another star face The Tribal Chief at WrestleMania.

It was none other than The People’s Champion Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson. Now, there is a huge debate going on social media as fans are debating between Rhodes and The Rock. Former NXT North American Champion Dominik Mysterio now has joined in on the conversation.

As mentioned earlier, The Great One returned to the Blue Brand this past Friday Night and had a stare-down with Roman Reigns. Earlier, in the Day One episode of Raw, The Great One had planted the seeds for this rivalry when he claimed he wanted to sit at the Head of The Table. Following his return, Rock took to Instagram to thank the WWE Universe for their support.

“FEEL the mana So f#%king blown away by this insane energy from the People. Undeniable – and one of the loudest ovations I’ve ever experienced in my career. Thank you Birmingham, Alabama, thank you WWE, thank you to the man himself, Cody Rhodes – and most importantly, thank you to the People. Our connection is unbreakable and our bond is ELECTRIC. ~ People’s Champ ps, tough skin, love the passion,” The Rock posted.

Dominik Mysterio took to Twitter to claim that he neither wants Rhodes or The Rock but rather wants to see Damian Priest in the main event of WrestleMania.

“@CodyRhodes = Ass @TheRock = Cheeks #WeWantSeñorMIB,” Dominik Mysterio tweeted.
