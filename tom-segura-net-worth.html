Tom Segura: Career

The Los Angeles-based standup comedian began his stand-up career shortly after graduating from Lenoir-Rhyne University.

For the first few years, he kept his day jobs while performing stand-up comedy in the evenings.

The comedian worked as an intern at Kopelson Entertainment.

Moreover, he had his first paid job as a logger.

In particular, he produced transcripts of reality shows such as Extreme Makeover and My Big Fat Obnoxious Boss.

Eventually, in 2007, Tom Segura formally began his standup comedy career by performing in several festivals.
