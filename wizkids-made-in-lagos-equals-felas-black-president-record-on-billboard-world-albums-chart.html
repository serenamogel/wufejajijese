Wizkids Made In Lagos Equals Felas Black President Record on Billboard World Album

Wizkid’s ‘Made In Lagos’ is still making giant strides globally and the latest feat it h as chalked is equally phenomenal.

The Afrobeat powerhouse, whose real name is Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun is now on par with his late compatriot Fela Anikulapo Kuti as far as a billboard chart is concerned.

Made In Lagos album has now equaled Fela’s, ‘The Best Of The Black President’ as the longest-running album on Billboard World Albums Chart, spending a total of 45 weeks on the chart.

Fela has his moment with The Black President compilation which he released in 1999 as a posthumous album. The Best of Fela Kuti is a 2-CD compilation album released by MCA Records. It dropped in the United States in 2000 as part of a reissue series of Kuti’s albums.

Billboard is an American music and entertainment magazine published weekly by the Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group, a division of MRC Media & Info. The magazine provides music charts, news, video, opinion, reviews, events, and style related to the music industry.
