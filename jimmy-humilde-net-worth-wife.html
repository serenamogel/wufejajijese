Introduction

Jimmy Humilde is an American Mexican music producer and promoter who is best known as the founder of the record label Rancho Humilde.

He represents popular acts, including Nataneal Cano and Junior H, and he promotes their music on his exceptionally popular YouTube channel.

Below, we explore the net worth of Jimmy Humilde and explain how this musical icon has earned most of his money. 

The Origin Story

Jimmy Humilde was born on July 21, 1980.

He grew up in Venice, California, before moving to Inglewood, where he spent most of his twenties.

He is bilingual and speaks both Spanish and English.

Humilde grew up listening to and loving music, and one of his inspirations from an early age was Chalino Sanchez.

He credits Sanchez with being the first musician to bring the corridos genre to him, and he remembers listening to his music when he was around 14 years old.

YouTube

One of the main sources of income for Jimmy is his YouTube channel, Rancho Humilde.

He promotes the music of the artists that he represents on his channel, providing them with a platform on which they can express their unique genre. 

On YouTube, Rancho Humilde has more than 6.8 million subscribers and has racked up more than 4.2 billion views since 2015.

It’s fair to say that Jimmy Humilde has made an incredible impact on the American-Mexican music scene, and his record label has enabled him to rack up his wealth. 

We know that YouTube pays its users for ad revenue on the platform, and the typical fee is somewhere in the region of $300,000 per 100 million views.

With more than 4 billion views on his channel, it’s fair to assume that Humilde has brought in around $12 million in ad revenue alone from YouTube.

However, given that his label represents different artists and the views are not for Humilde’s music, it’s not clear precisely how he pays his artists for playing their songs on his channel.

As their representative, there’s no doubt they will have struck a deal before the music is out there. 

Still, given the incredible success of Humilde’s YouTube channel, it’s clear that his success as a music producer and promoter online has helped him accumulate an impressive net worth. 

Away from YouTube, Humilde holds a contract with Clinq Music, which is a worldwide distributor of music recordings.

Like his success on YouTube, this deal has contributed significantly to his earnings over the years.  

Another income stream for Humilde is the Rancho Humilde online store, which stocks a range of merchandise, including corridos tumbados hats and tees, which are super popular with fans of the niche genre.

He also sells branded merchandise for the artists that he represents.

Humilde is also active on social media, and he has more than 65,000 followers. He mainly shares musical updates with his fans, but he also offers a glimpse into his private life, often posing with sports cars and at events that showcase his glamorous lifestyle. 

In October 2020, Humilde inked a musical agreement with Warner Music.
