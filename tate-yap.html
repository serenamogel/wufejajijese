Tate Yap was born in Guelph, Canada on October 2, 2002. TV actor is best known for his role as Agent O’Donahue on the series Odd Squad and in the subsequent film Odd Squad: The Movie. He won a Best Actor award for his performance in a film presented at the 48 Hour Film Project Toronto in 2012. He acted alongside Anna Cathcart in Odd Squad: The Movie.
