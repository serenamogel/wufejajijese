Fuse Recycling in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

There are plenty of great things you can do with your abilities in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, including fuse recycling. This guide will break down how you can fuse recycle and what that means for you when you are out there exploring Hyrule. Here is everything you need to know about fuse recycling in the game.

What is Fuse Recycling in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom?

If you use bursting materials such as Puffshrooms or Dazzlefruits and fuse them with a weapon, it means that you can use them again and again. However, this does only apply to two specific weapons. If you use it on these two weapons, you can use it as many times as you like. Using it on other weaponry means the material gets destroyed after a single strike. 

However, with the use of two particular weapons that you can obtain from a side quest, you can use fuse recycling in Tears of the Kingdom.

Which weapons can use Fuse Recycling in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom?

If you want to use fuse recycling, you will have to use the two following weapons:

  • Forest Dweller’s Sword
  • Forest Dweller’s Spear

You can obtain these weapons by completing Walton’s Treasure Hunt in the Great Hyrule Forest. You will find these two weapons by climbing on top of the Great Deku Tree. The coordinates for the sword are (0414, 2173, 0242.) If you want the Spear, the coordinates are (0502, 2196, 0229.)

Once you’ve found these weapons, you can abuse the power of fuse recycling as much as you want.

That’s everything you need to know about Fuse Recycling in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom.
